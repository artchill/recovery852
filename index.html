What We Do

Substance Recovery

Within Recovery852, we specialize in young adults, adults, and women grappling with substance abuse, encompassing but not limited to alcohol, methamphetamine, prescription medications, and more.

Process Addictions

Additionally, we provide support to clients contending with process addictions such as sex and gambling. Utilizing evidence-based education and therapeutic methodologies.

Meet the Owner

Grant Sanders

Registered Personal Recovery Coach

Grant Sanders is a registered personal recovery coach and member of the International Society of Substance Use Professionals (ISSUP).

Grant has over fifteen years of experience and over 3,000 hours of clinical experience working in addiction recovery. He has a Degree in Social Science. Grant is a US licensed and accredited, internationally certified addiction professional. He completed training in evidence-based counselling therapies like Motivational Interviewing for Addiction and Acceptance Commitment Therapy (ACT).
